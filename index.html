Deposits & Payment

  • • A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment.
  • • Remaining balance in cash or EcoCash.

Cancellations

  • • 48+ hours notice required to reschedule.
  • • Less than 48 hours forfeits deposit.
  • • No-shows will be charged in full.

Late Policy

  • • Arriving more than 30 minutes late may result in cancellation.
  • • If running late, please let us know ASAP.

Appointments

  • • No plus 1's allowed at your appointment.
  • • Children allowed only if receiving services.

Hair Prep

  • • Arrive with clean, detangled hair without oils.

Refunds & Care

  • • Deposits are strictly non-refundable.

Questions?

A non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking to secure your time slot. This deposit will be applied to your total service cost.
Yes, you can reschedule your appointment without penalty if you provide at least 48 hours' notice. Changes made with less than 48 hours' notice will result in the forfeiture of your deposit.
Please arrive with clean, detangled hair without any oils, leave-in conditioners, or heavy products. Standard blowdries are included in our services.
Standard braiding hair is typically provided, but for certain styles (like Boho or Sew-ins), you may need to provide your own human hair bundles. This will be specified when booking.
Depending on the style, braids can last 4–8 weeks with proper maintenance. We provide aftercare instructions to help you maximize the longevity of your style.
